It's Time to End Food Waste

So how does it work ?

Restaurant/Cafe : Makes a Post

  1. A Business has unsold food at the end of the day

    Instead of throwing this waste in the bin, it could be eaten by one of our users

  2. Business Posts on our Platform

    The business takes a picture of the food, adds a short description and posts on our platform

  3. Business gets notified if a user is coming to collect their food

    Users view posts, if they want to food detailed, they can notify the business that they are coming to collect the food

Users : View Available Food

  1. Users view posts

    Users can search for food based on location, they can then view all the posts that have been made in that area

  2. Indicate Interest in the food

    If a user wants the food described in a post, they can let the business know that they are on the way to collect it

  3. Users arrives to collect the food

    The process is complete, the user has some cheap fresh food and the business has reduced their waste
